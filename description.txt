This mod will make your NPC dialogue window better and more detailed, just like mobile version.
It completely overhauled NPC Dialogue Panel UI. Mods that also modified NPC dialogue panel might not work with this mod.
If issues happend, please leave a comment on this mod's Steam Workshop page.
